candle-gen is a Rust-based image generation framework built on the Candle ML library, implementing diffusion model pipelines. The candle-gen-chroma subproject specifically implements the Chroma image generation model in three variants (base, flash, HD), including beta-distribution-based noise scheduling, sigma schedule computation, and pipeline orchestration for text-to-image generation.
